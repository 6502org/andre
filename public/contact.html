My name is André Fachat, and these pages on http://www.6502.org show what I do for a hobby in my little spare time.

Mike Naberezny maintains the http://www.6502.org pages and has thankfully offered to host my pages on his site. Many thanks!

If you like this stuff, or have any questions about it, please let me know. I will, however, not answer any rants for example about how obsolete this technology is - it is not anyway, you may have more 6502s in your home than you think.

If you want to contact me, you can reach me via email on afachat@gmx.de. It has a strong spam filter and I am pretty busy, so it may take a while for me to answer, though.

I hope you enjoy these pages!
