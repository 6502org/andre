Other PET Documents

  • Pet FAQ - a lot of other PET information on Larry's portcommodore pages. Please see the menu there for the FAQ sections. He also has mode links, BASIC quick reference, and PET interface information.
  • PETdoc
  • pet-io.txt
  • Canonical list of Commodore products
  • The PET Prattle 1 for a brief historic overview
  • The ICPUG collection makes for a nice reading on PET and Commodore history...
  • The bombjack collection has a lot of scanned books for the PET. Michael Steil writes: 'There's also a pretty good memory/ROM map/cross-reference in Raeto West's 'Programming The PET CBM'; chapter 15, starting at p. 391.'
  • Scanned books for the PET
  • Lots of information about the MC6809 that is being used in the SuperPET.
