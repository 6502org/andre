Other PET sites

       PET index.

     * [30]Larry's Classic Computing Pages, including a [31]PET history page.
     * [32]The Commodore Knowledge Base

     * [37]IEEE488 related stuff for Commodore computers.
     * [38]Ruud Baltissen has a lot of info on Commodore-related hardware
       projects, not only for the PET.
     * [39]Commodore.ca with a lot of historical information, e.g. in [40]PET
       preview Oct 1978 or in [41]PET product overview.
     * [42]Commodore Manuals in "Commodore Heaven". English and Deutsch.

       including hardware hacks and software!
     * [44]CBMGAMES.DE
